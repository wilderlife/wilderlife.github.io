Summer is for hiking, winter is for skiing.

This blog will tell you everything you need to know about mountains from beginner to mid-experienced mountain lover. We will cover routes, basic safety measures, equipment, events and stories.

We will not cover rock climibing, or trail bazing, because we believe that you should do a proper course to do that - no internet blog post will ever teach you the skills need there.

We also want this to be a community run experience, so if you have stories you'd liket to share or topic you want us to cover, please drop us an email.
